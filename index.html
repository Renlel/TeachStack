FEATURES & FUNCTIONS

The "No-Formula"
Gradebook

Say goodbye to complex spreadsheet formulas and manual data entry. TeachStack instantly calculates running final grades the moment you input scores for quizzes, performance tasks, and major exams.

One-Click Attendance
Tracking

With quick-toggle buttons for Present, Late, or Absent, the system logs the data in real-time and automatically calculates cumulative attendance percentages for the entire roster. Never tally a manual attendance sheet at the end of the semester again.

Early Intervention Alerts

TeachStack's intelligent monitoring actively scans your classroom arrays to identify downward trends. The system automatically flags students with consistently dropping attendance or failing quiz scores, placing them on a prioritized dashboard so you can offer support exactly when it is needed most.
